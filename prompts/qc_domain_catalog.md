# QC Domain Catalog

Use this catalog to classify a submitted research question and choose search sources.

## life_sciences
- Includes: molecular biology, cell biology, microbiology, immunology, diagnostics, animal studies, wet-lab assay development.
- Recommended sources: PubMed, Europe PMC, Semantic Scholar, Crossref, protocols.io, Bio-protocol, Nature Protocols, JoVE, supplier technical notes.
- Use protocol search: yes.

## medicine_health
- Includes: clinical medicine, public health, epidemiology, therapeutics, diagnostics in patients, clinical biomarkers.
- Recommended sources: PubMed, Europe PMC, Semantic Scholar, Crossref, clinical guideline pages, Tavily web.
- Use protocol search: limited; prefer clinical study and guideline sources.

## chemistry_materials
- Includes: chemical synthesis, catalysis, analytical chemistry, polymers, batteries, semiconductors, materials science.
- Recommended sources: Crossref, Semantic Scholar, arXiv, Tavily web, supplier technical notes.
- Use protocol search: limited; only if the question asks for a lab protocol.

## climate_energy
- Includes: carbon capture, electrochemistry, climate modeling, energy systems, bioenergy, environmental engineering.
- Recommended sources: arXiv, Crossref, Semantic Scholar, Tavily web, standards or policy pages.
- Use protocol search: only for experimental laboratory or field protocols.

## computer_science_ai
- Includes: software, algorithms, AI/ML, databases, security, systems, human-computer interaction.
- Recommended sources: arXiv, Semantic Scholar, Crossref, Tavily web, conference pages, GitHub/docs when appropriate.
- Use protocol search: no.

## economics_social_science
- Includes: economics, business, education, policy, psychology, sociology, behavior, management.
- Recommended sources: Crossref, Semantic Scholar, Tavily web, SSRN or institutional pages when surfaced by search.
- Use protocol search: no.

## engineering_physics
- Includes: mechanical, electrical, civil, aerospace, photonics, quantum, instrumentation, robotics.
- Recommended sources: arXiv, Crossref, Semantic Scholar, Tavily web, standards/vendor documentation when appropriate.
- Use protocol search: no unless the question explicitly requires a lab method.

## manufacturing_robotics
- Includes: factory automation, industrial robots, robotic assembly, production lines, autonomous manufacturing, robotic handling of materials or products.
- Recommended sources: arXiv, Crossref, Semantic Scholar, Tavily web, IEEE/robotics conference pages, industrial automation documentation.
- Use protocol search: no.

## general_web
- Includes: questions that are not clearly scientific literature review tasks or do not fit the categories above.
- Recommended sources: Tavily web, reputable general sources, official documentation.
- Use protocol search: no.
